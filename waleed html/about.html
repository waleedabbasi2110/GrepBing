About:

Abasyn University Islamabad chartered by the Government of KPK and recognized by the Higher Education Commission (HEC), Pakistan. All the degree programs of the University have accreditations of their respective councils as well as HEC.


General Information:

HEC has granted NOC via letter No. 16-64/HEC/A&A/2010/401 to Abasyn University to open its campus at Islamabad. The University has established state of art facilities at Park Road, Chak Shahzad for the campus. Highly qualified teaching and non teaching staff have been hired. The campus has also established fully equipped labs with latest state of the art technologies and tools. The campus has also developed a well stocked library which has access to digital research databases, e-journals, e-books and e-reports.
